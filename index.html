About Us

Functional Interventions Therapy, a division of Aaron B Therapy Services LLC, is dedicated to helping children develop the essential skills they need to thrive. Based in Miami, our team of licensed therapists provides compassionate, evidence-based pediatric therapy services in a supportive and engaging environment.

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential. We work closely with families to create individualized treatment plans that address each child's unique needs, ensuring measurable progress and lasting results.

Our comprehensive approach combines Speech Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Physical Therapy, and Behavior Therapy to address every aspect of your child's development.

Our Services

Comprehensive pediatric therapy tailored to your child's unique needs

Speech Therapy

Communication is an essential skill that allows children to interact with their environment and build meaningful relationships. Our speech therapists help children overcome language barriers through personalized evaluation and treatment plans.

Common Conditions We Treat

  • Language delays and limited vocabulary
  • Articulation and pronunciation difficulties
  • Comprehension and processing challenges
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) communication needs

Therapeutic Techniques

  • Role-playing and social scenario practice
  • Interactive reading and storytelling activities
  • Pronunciation and articulation exercises
  • Technology-assisted learning tools

Key Benefits

  • Improved language and vocabulary skills
  • Increased confidence in communication
  • Better comprehension of spoken language
  • Enhanced social development and friendships

Occupational Therapy

Occupational therapy helps children develop the skills needed for everyday activities that are essential for growth and well-being. Through specialized activities, children learn to manage their environment and improve their academic and social performance.

Common Conditions We Treat

  • Fine motor skill delays (writing, cutting, manipulating objects)
  • Gross motor skill challenges (balance, coordination)
  • Sensory processing difficulties
  • Self-care and daily living skill delays

Therapeutic Techniques

  • Grip strengthening and fine motor exercises
  • Sensory integration activities
  • Art and craft-based skill building
  • Adaptive strategies for school tasks

Key Benefits

  • Improved fine and gross motor coordination
  • Greater independence in daily activities
  • Better academic performance
  • Increased self-confidence and autonomy

Physical Therapy

Physical therapy strengthens children's physical development, coordination, balance, and mobility. Our therapists use engaging activities to help children build strength and improve their overall physical health.

Common Conditions We Treat

  • Gross motor development delays
  • Balance and coordination difficulties
  • Muscle weakness and low tone
  • Mobility and movement challenges

Therapeutic Techniques

  • Outdoor activities and sports-based exercises
  • Balance and equilibrium training
  • Strength-building exercises and games
  • Functional movement activities

Key Benefits

  • Improved strength and physical endurance
  • Better balance and coordination
  • Enhanced ability to participate in play and sports
  • Long-term improvements in physical health

Behavior Therapy

Behavior therapy supports children's social and emotional development through evidence-based behavioral interventions. Our therapists help children develop positive behaviors, social skills, and emotional regulation strategies.

Common Conditions We Treat

  • Behavioral challenges and emotional regulation
  • Social skills difficulties
  • Attention and focus challenges
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) behavioral needs

Therapeutic Techniques

  • Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) strategies
  • Social skills training and role-playing
  • Positive reinforcement techniques
  • Emotional regulation and coping skills training

Key Benefits

  • Improved social interactions and relationships
  • Better emotional self-regulation
  • Reduced challenging behaviors
  • Enhanced school and community participation
